About Me

Abril P.M. is a Spanish film photographer, who loves and Spanish cultures in her daily life, as well as in her work. Her family originated in Spain, which led her to become a multicultural citizen before identifying with any nationality. In fact, this loss of identity provided her with the reason why she wanted to document the European lifestyle, to give a sense of what is to have a European identity. Currently studying desing in Spain at the Alicante School of Desing.

She started photography 4 years ago, with her grandfather’s film camera, a Nikon D90. Since then she hasn’t stopped experiencing the different methods that film photography could provide her. Failures and accidents have constituted a decisive process in her path as a photographer. However, from incidents, Abril has built her identity and aesthetic and portrays as well as social culture whihc has become dominant in her photography.
